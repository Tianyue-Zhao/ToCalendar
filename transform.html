Step 1

Please select the word document containing the homework calendar:

Step 2

Please enter the periods you need the calendars for in the following box.
Use single letters not separated by anything.
"BCF" would generate calendars for periods B,C and F.

Step 3

Please enter the name of the course your calendar is for.

Step 4

When you're done, click Convert and save the zip file that contains the homework calendars!

Step 5

Extract the data files from the zip

If you're a student...

Import the data files into your preferred calendar app, and you're all set!

If you're a teacher...

If your course has multiple periods, please share the data files with your students, and don't forget to tell them about it! If your course has a single period: Please go to your course in Schoology, and click on the calendar button:

Then scroll to the bottom, click on the "import" button, and import the data file for that period.

Congrats, you've imported the document!
